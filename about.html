Our Story

The Vision of Tollak

Founded in 2020 by the visionary Norwegian entrepreneur Tollak Birkeland, Tollak Beauty Shop was born from a unique blend of Scandinavian heritage and modern skincare science.

Growing up in the pristine fjords of Norway, Tollak was always fascinated by how his grandmother used local botanicals and natural ingredients to maintain her remarkably youthful skin despite the harsh Nordic climate. This early exposure to natural skincare solutions sparked a lifelong passion.

After studying biochemistry and spending years researching sustainable beauty solutions, Tollak discovered innovative ways to combine traditional Norwegian ingredients with cutting-edge skincare technology. His breakthrough came when he successfully developed a formula that harnessed the power of rare Arctic botanicals known for their resilience in extreme conditions.

Today, Tollak Beauty Shop stands as a testament to the perfect harmony between nature and science. Each product in our collection is carefully crafted using sustainable practices and ingredients sourced from the pristine Norwegian wilderness, bringing you the very best in natural beauty care.

"Beauty should be as pure and authentic as the Norwegian fjords themselves. Our mission is to help everyone discover their natural radiance through the power of nature's finest ingredients." - Tollak Birkeland
